Somatic Engagement


Traditionally presented orally, nursery rhymes are today used for entertainment and educational purposes in print and in digital media. Studies in neurobiology substantiate the importance for children’s development of the type of physical activity engaged in through enactment of nursery rhymes. Because of young children’s physical limitations, and because of (until recently) the minimal physical engagement provided by digital environments for young children, the physical engagement that has been an integral part of oral and print presentations of nursery rhymes for generations, exists only minimally in their presentation in today’s digital environments.
